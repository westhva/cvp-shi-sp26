If Enterprise feels like too big a conversation, Canva Business (25 seats, under $5k) is a faster path. Credit card, no procurement, no SSO/SCIM. Price increase already published. Good for smaller HOT/WARM accounts or as a stepping-stone conversation.

🔥 Template 1 — HOT Accounts (10+ paid seats)

Use for: Accounts with existing paid Canva seats. Goal is moving billing through SHI and formalising the agreement.
SubjectYour team’s Canva seats — moving billing through SHI
Hi [First Name], I’m your SHI rep — reaching out because I can see your organisation already has paid Canva seats, and I wanted to start a conversation about formalising that. Right now those seats are likely individual subscriptions outside your normal procurement flow. Moving them through SHI gives you: • Consolidated invoicing and POs under one agreement • SSO, SCIM provisioning, and admin controls • Brand kit management and approval workflows • SOC 2 compliance and enterprise-grade security • Visibility and control across the whole org In most cases the per-seat cost stays similar or comes down on a formal agreement. And right now there’s a pricing window worth acting on before Canva’s published increase takes effect. Worth 20 minutes with Canva’s team to see what a formal agreement would look like? [Your Name] [Your Title], SHI [Phone] | [Email]
Mention the specific number of paid seats if you know it — “I can see you have X paid seats” makes this feel personal, not generic.

🌊 Template 2 — WARM Accounts (high free usage)

Use for: Accounts with lots of free Canva usage. Goal is converting them to paid.
SubjectYour team is already using Canva — here’s what they’re missing
Hi [First Name], I’m your SHI rep. I noticed your team has been quite active on Canva’s free tier, and wanted to flag what a paid agreement could unlock. Right now your team is likely hitting free-tier limits: restricted templates, no brand controls, no admin visibility. The paid tier removes all of that: • Premium templates and content library • Brand kits — everyone works from the same approved assets • Background remover, video editing, AI tools • Admin dashboard and approval workflows • SSO and SCIM provisioning The other thing worth knowing: Canva has published a price increase online. The window to formalise at the current rate is short. Happy to set up a quick call with Canva’s team to walk through options. Does that make sense? [Your Name] [Your Title], SHI [Phone] | [Email]
If this is a smaller team, add a line about Canva Business: “For smaller teams, Canva Business (25 seats) is currently under $5k total — credit card, no procurement needed.”
