📋 How to connect your Google Sheet

  1. Open your Google Sheet (or create one — see the template below)
  2. Click File → Share → Publish to web
  3. Under "Link", choose Entire Document and Comma-separated values (.csv)
  4. Click Publish and copy the URL
  5. Open leaderboard.html, find SHEET_CSV_URL near the top, and replace the placeholder with your URL

Your sheet should have these columns (in order):
Rep Name  |  District  |  Calls Made  |  Emails Sent  |  Meetings Booked  |  Deal Registrations  |  Deals Booked
