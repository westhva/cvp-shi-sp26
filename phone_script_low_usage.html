📞 Phone Outreach Script

For Low Usage Accounts — 💡 POTENTIAL and 🔗 NEW

← Back to Dashboard

Before You Call

POTENTIAL accounts have some Canva free users but no paid seats. NEW accounts have little or no recorded usage. For both, this is an education and discovery call — plant the seed and aim for an intro call with Canva’s team.

Opening

You: “Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from SHI. I’m reaching out because we’ve been partnering with Canva to help companies get more out of their design and content tools. I wanted to grab two minutes to see if it might be relevant for your team.”

Discovery Questions

“Quick question — does your team do any kind of content creation? Presentations, social posts, marketing materials, internal docs, that sort of thing?”
“And what tools are you using for that today? PowerPoint, Adobe, anything like that?”

The Pitch

“What a lot of companies in your space are finding is that Canva Enterprise replaces four or five tools in one platform — presentations, documents, social design, video editing, whiteboards. And teams get up to speed in minutes, not weeks like with Adobe.”

Value Props to Work In

All-in-One

“Think of it as the platform where everyone on your team — not just designers — can create professional content. Sales decks, HR materials, training docs, you name it.”

Enterprise Grade

“On the enterprise side you get SSO, SCIM, SOC 2 compliance, brand management, and admin controls. It’s built for companies at your scale.”

Procurement

“And because we’re SHI, we handle the whole procurement side — one invoice, PO support, renewals managed for you.”

Common Objections

“We already use PowerPoint / Adobe / Google.”
“Totally — Canva isn’t about replacing everything at once. Most teams start using it alongside existing tools and find it gradually replaces them. A lot of companies actually reduce their overall software spend. Worth a 20-minute look?”
“We’re not looking for new software right now.”
“I hear you — timing is everything. But if there’s ever a point where you’re evaluating design or content tools, I’d love to make sure Canva is on the shortlist. Can I send you a quick overview to keep on file?”
“Send me an email.”
“Absolutely! Would it be okay if I also set up a quick intro call with Canva’s team? They can tailor it to your specific situation — no sales pitch, just a look at whether it fits.”

🏆 Goal: Book the Discovery Call

“What I’d suggest is a quick 20-minute intro with Canva’s team. No commitment, no sales pitch — just a chance to see if it’s relevant for you. Do you have 20 minutes in the next week or two?”

Close

“Perfect — I’ll send a calendar invite now and copy in the Canva team. Really appreciate your time today!”
💡 Tip: Even if they don’t book a meeting today, a call still counts for 3 pts and an email follow-up is 1 pt. Every touchpoint adds up!
